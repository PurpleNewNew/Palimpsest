# Decision Rubric

- `accept_risk`: evidence supports the finding and the remaining exposure is intentionally accepted.
- `mitigate_risk`: evidence supports the finding and the preferred next state is remediation or compensating control.
- `false_positive`: the current evidence materially contradicts the original finding.
- `needs_validation`: the signal is real enough to track, but the proof is not yet sufficient for a durable risk decision.
- `defer_risk`: the issue matters, but timing, scope, or dependencies require postponing the final decision.
